Meatballs

Description

Savory ginger meatballs served with herbal jasmine rice and lightly seasoned green beans.

Ingredients

  • 20oz of ground beef
  • 12oz green beans
  • 1 thumb ginger
  • 1 cup jasmine rice
  • 1/2 cup panko breadcrumbs
  • 4 Tbsp Mayo
  • 4 Tsp honey
  • 3 Tbsp Sour Cream
  • 2 Tsp Sriracha
  • 12 ml ponzu

Steps

  1. Set oven to 425 degrees.
  2. Rinse and dry produce
  3. Cut scallions into 1/4 inch pieces, seperating whites from greens and mince ginger
  4. Combine mayo, sour cream, ponzu and honey into a medium bowl. Add sriracha to taste.
  5. In a small pot combine rice, 1 1/2 cups water and salt.
  6. Bring to a boil, then reduce to a low simmer. Cook for 15 mins.
  7. Combine panko, beef, scallion whites, ginger and salt and pepper into a large bowl.
  8. Form into 10-12 1 1/2 inch meatballs and place on a lightly oiled baking sheet.
  9. Throw green beans on a serperate sheet, drizzle with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
  10. Roast meatballs on middle rack and beans on top for around 15 mins.
  11. Plate to bowls and serve!
